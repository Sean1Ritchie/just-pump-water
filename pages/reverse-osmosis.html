Reverse Osmosis

Ultra-pure water filtration through semi-permeable membrane technology.

The Technology

How Reverse
Osmosis Works

Reverse osmosis forces water through a semi-permeable membrane under pressure, removing up to 99% of dissolved salts, heavy metals, bacteria and other contaminants.

The result is ultra-pure water, ideal for drinking, cooking, aquariums and sensitive applications where water quality is critical.
