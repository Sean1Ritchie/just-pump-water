Our Story

Built on Experience.
Driven by Precision.

Just Pump Water (Pty) Ltd was founded on a single principle: water infrastructure is too important to be left to guesswork. Our founder, Eugene Barnard, brings over 20 years of hands-on experience in the pump and water treatment industry — from residential borehole installations to large-scale industrial water systems.

Based in the Brackenfell Industrial Area of Cape Town, we've built a reputation across the Western Cape as the specialists clients turn to when the job needs to be done right the first time. We've completed over 2,000 jobs — and every single one has been backed by the same commitment: technical excellence, honest advice, and dependable after-sales support.

Today, Just Pump Water supplies, installs and services pumps and water treatment systems for residential homeowners, farmers, commercial property managers, and industrial facilities across South Africa. We stock products from 10 of the world's leading brands — Grundfos, DAB, Pedrollo, Calpeda, Flygt, Lowara and more.
