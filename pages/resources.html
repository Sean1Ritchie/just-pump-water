FAQs

The right borehole pump depends on your borehole depth, casing diameter, required flow rate and total head. Our team will ask you these questions and recommend the correct submersible pump size and power rating. Contact us with your borehole specifications and we'll advise at no charge.

Most pumps benefit from an annual service — checking seals, bearings, impeller condition and electrical connections. Pumps handling water with high sediment or mineral content may require more frequent attention. We offer scheduled maintenance programmes for commercial and industrial clients.

Orange or rust-coloured water typically indicates dissolved iron in your borehole or municipal supply. This is very common in the Western Cape. An iron removal filter will resolve this completely. We supply and install iron removal systems sized to your flow rate and iron concentration.

Filtration removes physical particles and some chemicals from water. Reverse osmosis goes further — it forces water through a semi-permeable membrane that removes up to 99% of dissolved solids, including salts, heavy metals and bacteria. RO is typically used for drinking water; whole-house filtration is used for general water quality improvement.
